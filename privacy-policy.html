Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

WOMB ("we", "our", "the app") is a baby sleep sound application built by an independent developer. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

Anonymous usage analytics. We use PostHog to understand how the app is used. This includes events like which screens are viewed, which buttons are tapped, and which features are used. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. PostHog data is stored on servers located in the European Union.

Purchase information. If you subscribe to WOMB Premium, your purchase is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) through RevenueCat, our subscription management service. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card number, billing address, etc.). RevenueCat receives a transaction identifier and subscription status to manage your access to premium features.

Local preferences. The app stores your settings (volume levels, timer preferences, mixer configuration) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal contact information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect data about your baby or your family.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • PostHog (analytics): posthog.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions): revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play Store: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's privacy

WOMB is designed for parents, not for children. The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app plays soothing sounds to help babies sleep, but it is operated by the parent or caregiver, not by the child.

5. Data retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months, then automatically deleted. Local preferences are stored on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app.

6. Your rights

You can request deletion of your analytics data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or on this page. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last updated.
